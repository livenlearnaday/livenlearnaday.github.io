Bulk QR codes for scanning test

March 16, 2020

2020   ·   android   QR  

This post is just a collection of QR codes for the bulk QR scanning test (under the label AB001BBB12342020 - AB050BBB12342020).

QR scanning has been around for a long time and boofcv opensource project with available boofcv android app and boofcv sourcecode has taken it to the next level by introducing the bulk scanning of the QR codes. But wait, isn’t that feature already available in the zxing app? If you’ve used the feature before, you would know that QR bulk scannning by boofcv definitely has an edge over zxing. Nonetheless, zxing also supports barcode scanning which is not available on boofcv at the time of this post.
