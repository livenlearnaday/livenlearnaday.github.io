For the past week, I've been using corrugated plastic sheet/corriboard of various sizes as my little whiteboards. They are such gems! Just a light piece of corriboard + a  whiteboard marker pen + a piece of clothe/rag, viola, you've got yourself a reusable lightweight "whiteboard" set.
