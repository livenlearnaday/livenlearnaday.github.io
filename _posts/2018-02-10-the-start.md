It's official. I am quitting the role of credit analyst that I've in for almost a decade to fully commit my time to learn to code in about a month from now.

At near 40, I feel positively ancient. But, I still have another 20 years ahead of me before I reach the official retirement age. So I figure that the best time for any change would be NOW.

For now, I'll learn how to use javascript via freecodecamp.org site and to build a personal portfolio on github. Meanwhile, I will apply for Master Degree in computer science that is opened for registration during this period. To ensure that I can get into the course, I will be drilling as much computer knowledge as possible before the exam in May 2018. I know that I'm late in the game but the good thing here is that.....the game of life never ends.

"A journey of thousand miles begins with a single step." Lao-tzu.

"千里之行，始于足下。”  道德经

This is the start of another chapter of my life. Being single itself is a blessing in disguise. Challenges, disappointment and triumphant moments will sure to come. For now, I wish success to everyone. (This is my lame attempt at poetry. One is never too old to try. ^^).
